What is PotsBox?

Your own, personal network.

POTS means Plain Old Telephone Service — the elegant, electro-mechanical system that defined human communication for a century.

PotsBox lets you plug in real rotary and push-button phones and hear dial tones, operators, jokes, weather, and services that feel astonishingly alive — just like calling AT&T, 411, or an operator in 1975.

No screens. No apps. Just a phone, a wire, and a voice on the other end.
